Arkpedia

The wiki for Ark: survival evolved

Rex

The rex has a lot of health and stamina but is very
slow in water which makes them easy to attack from
below.

Spino

The spino live near water-areas and will stay there.
If the spino attacks you can just run away from their watersource to get away. Do not try to escape them by running into water as they are very fast underwater. They are strong, fast and have a fair amount of health.

Dodo

The dodo is a creature that moves around with little
speed and can be found on the beaches, hills and
forests around the island. If they are attacked they will
not fight back even if their egg is taken.

Raptor

The raptor is a dinosaur that will attack you on sight.
They attack creatures smaller or the same size as themselves.
When they are attacking in a pack of 3 or more they get a
pack buff and with their high speed they can be hard to escape. If you ever get attacked by a raptor you should run into water or run behind a door and close it.

Carnotaurus

The carno will attack humans but they will prioritize
other dinosaurs. It lives mainly on flat, clear ground where
it can use its speed. It does not mind running away from larger predators instead of fighting.
